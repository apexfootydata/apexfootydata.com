Privacy

Last updated: 2 July 2026

The short version

FPL Apex has no accounts, no sign-ups, and no database of users. We don't sell, share, or monetise personal data. Full stop.

Your FPL Team ID

When you enter your FPL Team ID, your squad is fetched directly from the official Fantasy Premier League API in your browser (via a proxy that adds no tracking). Your team and any plans you build are processed on your device and saved only in your own browser's local storage — never on our servers. Clearing your browser data removes them completely. Note that FPL team information (team name, points, rank) is already public via the official FPL API for anyone who knows a Team ID.

Analytics

We use Cloudflare Web Analytics and Google Analytics 4 to measure traffic — pages visited, rough location, device type. This tells us which tools people use; it isn't tied to your FPL identity. Where consent is required, it's collected on first visit.

Cookies & local storage

Beyond the analytics above, the site uses your browser's local storage for convenience only: remembering your Team ID and saved transfer plans between visits, on your device.

Questions or deletion requests

Email contact@fplapex.com. Since we hold no personal data server-side, "deletion" usually just means clearing your browser storage — but write to us and we'll help either way.
